OUR MOVEMENT

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

A Movement Towards a Cleaner India

Mission Overview

Launched on 2nd October 2014 by the Government of India, Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Clean India Mission) stands as one of the world's largest sanitation and cleanliness campaigns. This transformative initiative aims to eliminate open defecation, improve waste management, and foster hygiene awareness across India. With over 500 million lives impacted, it has fundamentally transformed communities, promoting sustainability and building a cleaner, healthier future for generations to come.

Read Full History on Wikipedia

Journey of Transformation

2014

Launch of Swachh Bharat Abhiyan with ambitious goals to transform India's sanitation landscape.

2017

Declaration of India as Open Defecation Free nation, marking a major milestone in public health.

2019

Swachh Bharat 2.0 launched focusing on waste management and sustainable environmental practices.

2024

Continued efforts toward a fully clean and sustainable India with community participation.
